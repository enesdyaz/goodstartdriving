Getting your license

The GDT&L system will make you better prepared for the road ahead.

  • Step 1. Learner Permit

    (Minimum Age 16)

    A theory test / Eyesight Test
    If you pass you will be permitted to drive a car under supervision
    with lerner's permit and learner guide and log book

  • Step 2. Hazard Perception Test

    (16 years and 6 months)

    Begin to record your minumum 50 hours of superised driving(include 5 hours of night time driving) with your instructor until a mandatory 6 month period has lapsed since the issue date of your learner's permit. and you can undertake Hazard Perception Test.

  • Step 3. practical driving Assessment

    (17 years old)

    If you completed a minumum of 50 hours of supervised driving experience and passed a HPT, You can have Practical Driving Assessment.

  • Step 4. Provisional License

    Congratulations, you now have your 'P' plate

    Red P plate for 6 months and swap to the green P for the remaining time on your provisional license.
